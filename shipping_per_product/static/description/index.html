Shipping Per Product

Odoo Shipping Per Product Module is a perfect way to make your customers select delivery methods and calculate delivery amount for each product separately. This provides an ease to split the delivery according to the service availability at customers location.

Information

User Guide
https://webkul.com/blog/odoo-shipping-per-product/

Why enable shipping per product facility?

Because customers can avail the delivery services as per the availability in the customer's location.

  • You can add different delivery methods for each product.
  • Customers can pay delivery amounts as per the product only.
  • The delivery amount will be calculated for each product at the time of checkout.
How can you make the most out of this plugin? Benefits
  • Attracts customers as it provides delivery services as per the location.
  • Can set different amounts for different delivery methods.
  • Attract new users from areas where only selected delivery methods are available.
  • You can deliver the fragile items separately.
  • Customers can split the delivery of the items without splitting the order.
  • Can assign different delivery methods per product separately.
  • Customers can select different delivery methods in the same order itself.
  • Adds a "dynamic delivery method" in the Sales Order order form.
  • Add delivery methods to the product as shipping options for the product.
  • Makes you stand out from other competitors as you provide different shipments in the same order.

Detailed Features List

Assign different delivery methods accordingly

  • Admin can add different delivery methods for each product accordingly.
  • These delivery methods will be available on the website for this product from which a customer can choose only one.

Enables customers to choose delivery methods as per their wish

  • Customers can choose a delivery method for each product separately
  • The delivery price will be calculated for each product separately.

Avoid multiple orders placement

  • It allows you to deliver different products in the same sales order rather than placing multiple orders.
  • You can deliver fragile items separately with full security.

Adds a new delivery methodAdds a new delivery method

  • You can Add a new delivery method- "dynamic delivery method" in the Sales Order form.
  • It allows you to add delivery methods to the product to show as shipping options for the product on the website.
